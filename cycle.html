The Great Chain of Being is a cyclic arrangement of plastic processing machines. The arrangment shreds plastic into thin flakes, extrudes the flakes into a 1/4 inch wire, chops it into 1 inch sections, then conveys the sections back into the shredder, starting the processs again. No material is lost, gained, or yielded.

steel, aluminum, PLA, neoprene - 2024
