Minor info on Myself
Jonathan Dinetz is a multimedia artist based in Providence, RI. His practice is focused on investigating lineages of power manifesting in contemporary culture, utilizing speculation to expose these lineages and their alternatives. The objects he presents aim to plasticize realities conceived as banal or invisible, recasting them as tangible and profoundly strange.

Jonathan was born in San Francisco and is currently studying furniture design as a junior at the Rhode Island School of Design.
